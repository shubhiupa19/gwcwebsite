Mission Statement

NYU Tandon's premier undergraduate student association dedicated to closing the gender gap in technology and changing the image of what a programmer looks like and does.

Join GWC HQ with the code: CL49269 to help us get more funding and resources!

Supporting women in tech at the collegiate level

academically

College Loops facilitate on–campus opportunities and resources for students to learn more about programming technologies and how to code.

professionally

College Loops provide college–aged women with exclusive opportunities to connect and build relationships with industry professionals and Girls Who Code corporate partners.

socially

College Loops connect young women on campus who are interested in tech to build a supportive community that helps them persist and succeed in the field.
